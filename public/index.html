FAQ

Quick answers to the stuff people ask first.

What materials do you print?
PLA for most items, PETG for tougher/heat-resistant use, and TPU for flexible parts.
How fast is turnaround?
Depends on size and quantity. Small items can be same-day; sets are typically 2 to 5 days.
Do you do bulk orders?
Yes. Bundles and repeats are where pricing gets good.
Can you print any model I find online?
Only if the design license allows commercial use, or if you own the rights.
